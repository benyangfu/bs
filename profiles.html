Character Profiles - Character Descriptions (Act 2)

Here are the character profiles and descriptions for the characters in the play Rizz-eo and Rizz-iet. (IN ACT 2)

Rizz-eo

Name: Rizz-Eo

Chronically online depressed kid, who wishes to have a girlfriend. He speaks in social media slang.

Characteristics:

  • Chronically online, so he speaks weirdly
  • Weird kid
  • Son of Montague Inc
  • Depressed, until he meets Rizziet
  • Becomes more rizzy when he meets Rizziet
  • Wants Rizziet to be with him
